{{define "content"}}

Add standard branding and tags to an image.

  • JPG, PNG, and GIF images are supported, though this is primarily intended for GIFs.
  • A maximum of 20MB per upload applies and at least one tag is required.
  • Tags are reduced to lowercase letters and numbers.
  • The font size is variable based on the image height with a range of 16-32 points.
{{end}}
